What we do

  1. Taking over vacant possession from developer and carrying out inspection and handling ratification of the property.
  2. Providing renovation solution to the investor (optional).
  3. Marketing of the property
  4. Background screening and proper registration of the tenant for the safety and security of the community.
  5. Checking in and out of the tenant, with proper inventory checking.
  6. Managing of leasing
  7. Providing regular inspection to the property, in order to make sure the property is in good condition at all time.
  8. Providing 24 hours technical support and handling of emergency matters.
  9. Solving conflict amongst tenants and community.
  10. Collecting of rental.
  11. Providing regular cleaning services and pest control to the property.
  12. Touching up of the property (when necessary), by house or room, upon the expiry of the tenant’s leasing term.
